Instructions

This is just a student project. This work is not related to ETS (creators of the GRE) in any way.
1. This game tests your knowledge of vocab necessary for the vocab section of the GRE.
2. Choose letters to make up a word that has the same meaning as one of the hints.
3. Guess right all the three words to move ahead.
4. If you selected the wrong letter, click on "Reset Selection".
5. To reset the game, click on restart game.
6. If you cannot guess all te three words, then click on "give up".
7. "Restart game" and "give up" will reset your score to zero(0).
8. You get 10, 25, 50 points for each "easy", "medium" and "hard" word answered, respectively.
9. If you answer all three words for a given difficulty, then you move ahead to the next difficulty.
10. If you give up, you move down by one difficulty level.
